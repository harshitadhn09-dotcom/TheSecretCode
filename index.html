The Secret Code Game

Welcome to Mystery Number! A secret number is hiding somewhere between 1 and 100. Your mission is to uncover it using as few guesses as possible. After each guess, you'll receive a hint: 📈 Too High — aim lower. 📉 Too Low — aim higher. 🎉 Correct! — you've found the mystery number. Think carefully, use the clues wisely, and see how quickly you can solve the mystery. Are you ready to begin?
